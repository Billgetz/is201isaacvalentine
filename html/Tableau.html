My Favorite Sport

In the 2024 Olympic gold medal game, Guerschon Yabusele posterized LeBron James with a ferocious dunk. Late in the second quarter, with Team USA up 40-33, Yabusele drove baseline past Kevin Durant, met LeBron in the paint, and powered through him for a one-handed slam. LeBron hit the floor, a foul was called, and the French crowd erupted as the lead shrank to 40-35. It was a brutal, momentum-shifting highlight in a game the U.S. ultimately won 98-87.
